§ 01 — Channel Identity

Teen naam, ek soch.

Pick the one that feels like YOU saying it on camera for the next five years. Pronunciation test: Can your mom say it without stumbling?

Recommended
Option A

The Data Pandit

Authority + desi warmth. "Pandit" signals expertise, not religion. Works in search, stands out on a thumbnail, easy to brand as a logo.

Casual
Option B

Data Adda Hindi

"Adda" = hangout. More community-first, less authority-first. Good if you want the long-haul "friend who teaches" vibe.

Direct
Option C

Data Engineer Banao

Pure SEO play. Literally tells viewers the promise. Lower brand ceiling, higher early-stage click-through. Risky long-term.

§ 02 — Audience & Positioning

Kaun dekhega, aur kyun.

The sharper your audience, the faster the algorithm finds them. Broad channels die. Specific channels scale.

Your 3 viewer personas

  • 01
    The Switcher — 2–5 years in IT (testing, support, development), earning ₹6–12 LPA, wants to jump to data engineering for ₹20+ LPA. Tier-2 city, engineering degree, English-capable but thinks in Hindi.
  • 02
    The Fresher — Final year B.Tech / BCA / MCA student. Tired of LeetCode grind, looking for a path with better job security than pure SDE. Needs roadmap, not theory.
  • 03
    The Analyst — 1–3 years as data analyst or BI developer. Hits ceiling at ₹10 LPA. Wants to learn Spark, Airflow, cloud to move up. Already knows SQL well.
Your one-line pitch

Main aap ko data engineering sikhata hun jaisa ek senior aap ko office mein sikhata — bina jargon ke, real projects ke saath, Hindi mein.

§ 06 — Launch Week Checklist

Record karne se pehle.

Do not skip any of these. Most first videos fail not because the content was bad, but because these basics were rushed.

Gear & Setup

Audio first, video second. Boya M1 lavalier (~₹1,500) or Maono PD-200 USB mic. Viewers forgive bad video, never bad audio.budget: ₹1,500 – ₹6,000
Phone camera works fine. iPhone 12+ or any decent Android. Natural light from a window, face it directly. No ring light needed initially.budget: ₹0
Background. Bookshelf or one clean wall with ONE frame. No clutter. Don't show your bed.signal: professionalism
Editing software. CapCut (free, mobile) or DaVinci Resolve (free, desktop). Don't buy Premiere in month 1.learning curve: 1 weekend

Content & Launch

Record the first 3 episodes before publishing episode 1. This removes the "next video ka pressure" and keeps quality consistent.rule: inventory = confidence
One topic per video. "Data Engineering kya hai AND salary AND roadmap" = disaster. Split into three videos.principle: one promise, one payoff
Publish Monday 8 AM IST. Office-goers ka metro commute time. Set schedule and don't break it for 12 weeks.consistency > virality
Reply to every comment for the first 30 days. Algorithm tracks engagement signals. Comments = subscribers = recommendations.time cost: 30 min/day
Ignore views for 90 days. Track watch-time and retention instead. Views are a lagging indicator; retention is a leading one.metric: avg. view duration
Ab jaao, record karo.
